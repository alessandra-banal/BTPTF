Beneath the Parisian Tie Factory is a short documentary celebrating Audio Foundation
It centers around Audio Foundation, a unique organization located in an unassuming building beneath a Parisian tie factory, nestled in between Myers Park and St. Kevin’s arcade, deep in the heart of the vibrant K road arts community. Established to support, promote and preserve all creative practices relating to sound, Audio Foundation has become a seminal part of the audio community in Auckland. This documentary briefly profiles the directors, sound artists, and workshop hosts that make it all possible, as it follows the creative endeavors of emerging sound artist Daisy Wells and the way she engages with this unique community space.
